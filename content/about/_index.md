## What is Overlooted?

**Overlooted** is a cozy dungeon crawler where the victory condition isn't slaying the boss — it's hauling home a full cart of loot.

You explore handcrafted dungeons filled with treasure, traps, and the occasional very upset skeleton. New tools unlock as you progress, opening paths to deeper and more dangerous areas — Metroidvania-style. Your cart has limited space, so every run is a puzzle: what do you take? What do you leave behind? Can you make it back to the surface before the dungeon fights back?

Think **inventory management** meets **Metroidvania exploration**, wrapped in a warm, hand-drawn art style.

---

## Core Pillars

{{< lead >}}
**Cozy but tense.** The dungeons are dangerous, but the mood is never oppressive. Warm lighting, gentle music, and a world that feels lived-in.
{{< /lead >}}

**Cart-filling strategy** — your cart is your score, your inventory, and your lifeline. Every item has weight, size, and value. Packing it efficiently is half the game.

**Handcrafted dungeons** — every room, shortcut, and secret is designed by hand, with new tools gating access to deeper areas as you progress.

**No combat requirement** — you *can* fight, but running, hiding, and outsmarting enemies is just as valid.
